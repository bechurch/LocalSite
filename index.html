Local

What’s fun to do around here?

Local is an iPhone app that gathers activities and experiences located around the user, displaying them ranked by popularity and satisfaction. It provides everything there is to do in your area, so that you never miss out on a fun experience again just because “No one told you.”

Whether you’re a seasoned veteran, or a total newbie, Local helps you get past the hotel info desk and out into the real world, where the real fun is!

Get the information you need to start your adveture

We don't want you to waste any time in finding the perfect experience for you. That is why as soon as you select an activity we provide you with access to all the information you need to get out and explore your destination.

Right away you can see a description and images to help you make your choice. Call and Email right from the app to inquire further and reserve your spot. Finally we also provide you with directions all the way to your next adventure.

The Future of Traveling

Local is a small app with a big dream. We want to change the way people explore the world around them and are always pushing towards this goal. This app will always be under development as long as us at Local keep finding new ways to provide value to the adventurers of the world.

As we push forward we are already looking ahead at a long list of features we'd like to implement:

  • Local Secrets
  • Peer reviews of activities
  • In App Messaging
  • Local Guides
  • Online Booking

If you want to keep updated on our progress towards our goals subscribe to our mailing list and don't worry we promise to never spam you.
